# Commercial Licensing

KYCortex Agents is available under a dual-license model.

## Open-Source Distribution

The public repository and open-source distribution remain available under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. See [LICENSE](LICENSE) for the full open-source license text.

## Commercial Licensing

KYCortex also offers separate commercial licensing for organizations that need terms outside the AGPL model.

Typical reasons to discuss a commercial license include:

- embedding KYCortex Agents into a proprietary product or internal platform
- operating a closed-source hosted service based on modified versions of the framework
- distributing private forks without AGPL-style source-code obligations
- requiring commercial support, negotiated terms, or contractual assurances beyond the open-source license

This file is an overview of the commercial licensing path. It is not the full commercial contract.

## Requesting Commercial Terms

For commercial licensing inquiries, contact Alexandre Andrade at alex@kycortex.com.

When reaching out, include:

- your organization name
- your intended deployment model
- whether you expect to modify the framework
- whether you need redistribution rights, private SaaS operation, or support terms

## Contributor Rights Note

Dual licensing depends on the project maintainer having the necessary rights to distribute the relevant code under both open-source and commercial terms.

If future external contributions are accepted for commercially distributed builds, maintainers may review contributor rights on a case-by-case basis and request an explicit written rights grant when needed.
