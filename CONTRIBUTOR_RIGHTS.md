# Contributor Rights Policy

This repository is maintained under a dual-license business model.

## Public Open-Source Distribution

The public repository is distributed under the GNU Affero General Public License v3.0. See [LICENSE](LICENSE).

## Commercial Distribution

KYCortex may also distribute the codebase under separate commercial terms. See [COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md](COMMERCIAL_LICENSE.md).

## Why Contributor Rights Matter

Dual licensing only works if the project maintainer has the necessary rights to distribute the contributed code under both the open-source license and separate commercial terms.

## Current Contribution Policy

- Small documentation, test, and maintenance contributions may be reviewed under the repository's normal pull-request workflow.
- Maintainers may require an explicit written rights grant before merging contributions that are intended to ship in a commercial distribution.
- Maintainers may decline contributions when the contributor's rights to submit the work are unclear.
- Contributors must not submit code they do not have the right to license.

## Employer And Client Work

If you contribute on behalf of an employer, customer, or another organization, you are responsible for confirming that you have authority to submit the contribution.

## Future Process

KYCortex may introduce a more formal contributor-rights workflow for external contributions if that becomes necessary to support commercial distribution at scale.

Until then, maintainers may handle rights confirmation on a case-by-case basis.
